Heart Compass - Emotional Coaching Services (Israel)

Core Method: M.S.E (Mapping, Clearing, Independence)

The M.S.E method is a neurobiological and philosophical approach to releasing deep-seated emotional patterns. It focuses on identifying the root of "stuckness," clearing emotional noise, and building independence.

Services Provided:

1. Individual Emotional Coaching

•
Description: Releasing patterns and barriers from the root, dealing with anxiety, procrastination, and low self-confidence.

•
Target Audience: Individuals interested in personal development and breakthroughs.

•
Focus Areas: Anxiety, overthinking, perfectionism, fear of failure.

2. Couples Coaching (M.S.E Method)

•
Description: Rebuilding communication, dealing with conflicts, and building renewed intimacy by addressing individual patterns first.

•
Target Audience: Couples in crisis or those interested in deepening their connection.

•
Process: Initial individual mapping for each partner, followed by joint sessions.

3. Career & Business Coaching

•
Description: Dealing with self-doubt, fear of failure, and procrastination in the world of work.

•
Target Audience: Entrepreneurs, self-employed, and managers.

•
Goal: Moving from "intellectual understanding" to practical action in the business.
